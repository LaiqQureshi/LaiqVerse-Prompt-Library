The Person
Laiq Ahmed Qureshi
Creative Director & Digital Strategist · Karachi, Pakistan

With over 20 years of experience in advertising, branding, and digital strategy, Laiq Ahmed Qureshi sits at an unusual intersection — part Creative Director, part AI experimenter, part observer of how machines are reshaping imagination itself.

An alumnus of the National College of Arts (NCA), Lahore, Laiq brings a trained visual sensibility to everything he does — from campaign strategy to AI-generated editorial art. His work spans clients including Prestige Communication and Gravity, with a career rooted in the belief that emotion and information are never opposites.

LaiqVerse is his personal brand and ongoing experiment — a space where AI, graphic design, and visual arts collide. The tagline says it plainly: "I direct machines to imagine." He prefers the title Imagineer.

He writes about AI not as a technologist, but as a creative professional watching the shift happen in real time — cutting through hype to find what actually changes, what stays human, and what that means for the people who make things for a living.
